About V5 Medical LTD

Professional supplier of high-quality medical and dental consumables serving global healthcare markets

Our Company

V5 Medical LTD is a company that sells all kinds of medical devices. The company is located in Kunshan City, Jiangsu Province, which is one of the top 100 counties and one of the sanitary cities in China. The company is mainly engaged in the sales and service of all kinds of medical equipment, devices and medical consumables.

The company always adheres to the business philosophy of "creating wealth, repaying the society, serving the staff, and contributing to the cause of human health", actively and effectively promotes the modern enterprise management, implements the business policy of "continuously providing customers with first-class service", and devotes itself to becoming a well-known enterprise in the field of medical equipment sales, and walks out of a standardized road of development in the increasingly fierce competition in the market to become a leading company among its counterparts in and outside of the area.

Adhering to the concept of "people-oriented, respect for science, focus on quality, legitimate business, and common development", we advocate the corporate culture of "rigorous, harmonious and progressive." Our business philosophy is: talent as the fundamental, market-oriented, quality as the core, service as the purpose, and efficiency as the target.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality medical and dental consumables that improve healthcare outcomes worldwide while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and customer service.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized leader in medical consumables supply, driving innovation and excellence in healthcare product manufacturing and distribution.

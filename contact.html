Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with V5 Medical LTD

Our MOQ varies by product category. For most standard medical consumables, the MOQ is 10,000-50,000 pieces per item. For customized products, MOQs may be higher depending on specifications. We're flexible for trial orders and can discuss options that work for your business needs.

Yes, we offer product samples for quality evaluation before placing bulk orders. Sample fees may apply depending on the product type and quantity requested. Shipping costs for samples are typically paid by the customer. Contact our sales team to request specific samples for your evaluation.

We accept various payment methods including T/T (bank transfer), L/C (letter of credit), and PayPal for smaller orders. Standard payment terms are 30% deposit with order confirmation and 70% balance before shipment. For established partners, we can discuss more favorable payment terms based on order volume and history.

Lead times depend on product type, order quantity, and customization requirements. Standard lead time is 30-45 days after order confirmation and receipt of deposit payment. For urgent orders, we offer expedited production options for an additional fee. Our sales team will provide specific lead times based on your exact requirements.
